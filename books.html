Career of
Evil

This is the third book in the series that J.W. Rowling has written about the private detective Cormoran Strike, under the pseudonym Robert Galbraith. Although the course of crime investigation and the murder's way of thinking description is very interesting, the developing of the relationship between Cormoran and Robin, his secretary, is the best part. This book series has a television serie called C.B Strike.

Every Good Endeavour

The preacher of the Redeemer's Church, Timothy Keller, presents a biblical perspective on the work in this book. Basically, it shows how we can love God and our fellow man through work, and how work is an essential part of human life. I found especially interesting the explanation of how we reflect the image of God through work, and how we work so many times with wrong motivations and then we have frustration in our professional lives.

El Amor en los Tiempos de Cólera

Gabriel García Márquez is the most important writer in my country, and his most popular book, Cien Años de Soledad, is one of my favorite books. However, when I was in high school I read another book by him, called La Mala Hora, and this book was terribly boring for me. At this moment I am reading El Amor en los Tiempos de Cólera, and although I still don't have my final perception, for now I think I like this book.
