Mocha Rice Pudding

Find the original recipe in Fashionable Food

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Rice
  • 1 Cup Espresso (coffee)
  • 3 Cups Whole Milk
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 1/3 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon Cocoa
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Instructions

  1. Combine the whole milk, coffee, and rice into a medium-large pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, give it a good stir, and then reduce the heat to low. Cover and then simmer for 15 minutes.
  2. Remove the lid and continue cooking until the rice has plumped up and is tender, about 3-5 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the egg yolks with the cocoa powder, sugar, and vanilla extract. Add a scoop of the hot rice pudding and stir vigorously to temper the egg yolks. Pour the mixture back into the pot and stir to combine.
  4. Continue cooking for another minute or so, just until the pudding thickens up and everything is mixed together.
  5. Spoon the rice pudding into your desired serving dishes and cool before refrigerating to chill completely.
  6. Serve the rice pudding cold topped with grapes or other fruit you like, if desired.
